DayPath - plan your day

What is DayPath?

DayPath is an online day planner, which enables you to keep track of your tasks and take advantage of Pomodoro Technique by using PomoTimer. Sign up to save your DayPath and organise your tasks on every device, whereever you are.

How to use the DayPath?

Simply enter tasks, which you want to accomplish today and move them to the next stages. Switch to PomoTimer, to use built-in timer.

How does Pomodoro Technique work?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method, created by Francesco Cirillo for a more productive way to work and study. The technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. Each interval is known as a pomodoro, from the Italian word for 'tomato', after the tomato-shaped kitchen timer that Cirillo used as a university student.
